Glowing With God Fund

Glowing With God Fund is a nonprofit program connected to Hair By Gena INC. that helps selected women experiencing hair loss receive a sponsored mesh integration hair system based on financial need and available funding. Donations help purchase the hair system, while Hair By Gena INC. donates installation and continued care services for selected recipients.

Visit the Glowing With God Fund website to apply for the Crown Winner Program, learn how the process works, or support the fund.
