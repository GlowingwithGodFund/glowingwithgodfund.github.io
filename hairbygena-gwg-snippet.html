Glowing With God Fund

Help restore confidence, one crown at a time.

Glowing With God Fund is a nonprofit program created by Regina Barbato also known as Hair By Gena INC. that helps selected women experiencing hair loss receive a sponsored mesh integration hair system based on financial need and available funding. Donations help purchase the hair system, while Hair By Gena INC. donates installation and continued care services for selected recipients.
